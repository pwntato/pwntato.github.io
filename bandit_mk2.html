Bandit MK2 Rubber Band Gun

I designed the Bandit MK2 as a simplification of the original Bandit MK1. It has the advantage of being faster to print. The barrel is also modular so it can be extended to be arbitrarily long.

It has a more traditional trigger that rotates instead of the MK1's 1911 inspired trigger that pulls straight back. This causes a less smooth pull, but simplifies the mechanism.

You can get the OpenSCAD code by cloning the repo. You might need to play with the tolerances to get the right fit on your printer.

By default, it will generate the design view. Since this is a reasonable length print, comment out parts_for_design(); by adding // before it and remove the // before //all_parts_for_printing();.
