The J5 Project

Welcome to the J5 Project, a place to share my open source 3D Printable CAD designs.

Hi! I'm Jimmy Hendrix, welcome to my projects page!

I spend my days working on LivePlan.com, making fantastic business planning software. Then I split my free time between my awesome family and my side projects. My early side projects were interesting bits of software, but now I focus on CAD for 3D printing.

I first discovered 3D printers at Siggraph in my late teens and dreamed of one day having my own. That day finally came over a decade later when consumer 3D printers fell into my price range.

Then came the question: what now?

After some experimenting with different technologies, I found OpenSCAD. I could leverage my programming skills to make real things! That's just what I did.

From a lot of trial and error, I got familiar with the limitations of what consumer 3D printers are able to do well. It became my goal to design neat things that would be easy to print reliably. I launched this page to share those projects with you.

Take a look around and follow me on twitter for updates as I add more projects.

Thanks for stopping by!
